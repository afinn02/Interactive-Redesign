Distribution of Public Schools by County and Title I Status in NYC-Area (2015-2016)

Caption: This starburst chart demonstrates where public schools are located throughout the NYC area, the Title 1 status of the schools, and the overall student minority population within the county. Schools with higher levels of marginalized student populations (black, hispanic, native) are more likely to be Title 1. Within integrated regions such as Essex, Nassau, Suffolk, Westchester, and Hudson, Title 1 schools have most of the minority population within the county. Non-title 1 schools tend to have more white and asian students. This data was sourced from: https://nces.ed.gov/datatools/
